Our Story

Hugs in a Bag began with a simple idea: no one should feel alone during their hospital stay. What started as a small group of students making cards has grown into an international movement of compassion.

Founded in [Year], we've expanded from a handful of volunteers to a global network of caring individuals who believe in the power of human connection. Every craft we create, every note we write, carries with it the warmth of human kindness.
