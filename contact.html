Frequently Asked Questions

How can I become a volunteer?

+

We're always looking for passionate volunteers! Visit our Get Involved page to learn about different volunteer opportunities and fill out our application form. We accept volunteers from all around the world and will work with you to find a role that matches your skills and interests.

What materials do you need for donations?

+

We appreciate donations of craft supplies including: cardstock and decorative paper, markers and colored pencils, yarn for knitting projects, fabric for sewing crafts, stickers and embellishments, and small toys or comfort items. We all appreciate your love and help.

How do you deliver the comfort kits to hospitals?

+

We partner directly with hospitals and healthcare facilities. Our hospital relations team coordinates with each facility to understand their needs and arrange deliveries. We ensure all items meet hospital safety and hygiene standards before delivery.

Can I start a Hugs in a Bag chapter in my area?

+

Yes! We encourage the creation of local chapters to expand our reach. Contact us via email to discuss starting a chapter in your area. We'll provide guidance, resources, and support to help you get started and connect with local hospitals.

Are donations tax-deductible?

+

As a student-led initiative, we're currently working on obtaining official non-profit status. Please contact us for the most up-to-date information about tax-deductible donations and receipts.

How can hospitals request comfort kits?

+

Healthcare facilities interested in receiving comfort kits can email us directly. Please include information about your facility, patient demographics, and estimated needs. Our hospital relations team will work with you to establish a partnership.

Do you accept virtual volunteers?

+

Absolutely! Many of our volunteers work remotely. Note writers, translators, and website developers can all contribute from anywhere in the world. We use online collaboration tools to stay connected and coordinate our efforts.
