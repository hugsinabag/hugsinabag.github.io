Get Involved

Join our mission to spread warmth and comfort

Make a Difference Today

Thank you for your interest in joining Hugs in a Bag - Packed with Love! As a volunteer-driven initiative, we rely on compassionate individuals like you to bring comfort to hospital patients around the world.

Whether you're crafty, love writing, speak multiple languages, or have technical skills, there's a place for you in our community. Every contribution, no matter how small, makes a meaningful impact in someone's healing journey.

You can volunteer as many time as you want. No pressure on the time commitment! Even small actions are great help to the community.

Volunteer Opportunities

📝

Note Writers & Translators

Create heartfelt messages that bring hope and comfort to patients. Help us reach more people by translating notes into different languages.

⏰
Recommended Time Commitment: 2-3 hours per week
💡
Skills Needed:
  • Compassionate writing
  • Creativity and empathy
  • Language skills (for translators)
  • Cultural sensitivity
🎯
Responsibilities: Write uplifting messages, translate existing notes, participate in monthly writing workshops
🎨

Designers & Crafters

Use your creative skills to design beautiful cards and craft handmade items that bring joy to patients during their recovery.

⏰
Recommended Time Commitment: 3-5 hours per week
💡
Skills Welcomed:
  • Card making and paper crafts
  • Knitting or crocheting
  • Sewing and fabric crafts
  • Digital design (optional)
🎯
Responsibilities: Create handmade items, participate in crafting sessions, help develop new craft ideas
💻

Project Reporting & Website Development

Help us share our story and impact through digital platforms, documentation, and technical development.

⏰
Recommended Time Commitment: 3-4 hours per week
💡
Skills Needed:
  • Web development (HTML/CSS/JS)
  • Content writing
  • Data analysis and reporting
  • Social media management
🎯
Responsibilities: Maintain website, track impact metrics, create reports, manage online presence
