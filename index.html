Our Mission

Hugs in a Bag is a student-based community service initiative aimed to bring emotional comfort to patients through small, meaningful gestures of encouragement.

We create comfort kits filled with handmade cards featuring uplifting messages and small handcrafted items such as gloves, hats, and toys. By partnering with hospitals across the US and internationally, we deliver these kits to provide warmth and hope to patients during their healing journey.

Every note written, every craft created, and every kit delivered represents a virtual hug - a reminder that someone cares.
