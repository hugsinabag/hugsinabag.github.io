Silent Weight of Hospital Anxiety: No One Should Face It Alone

The hospital can be an overwhelming place. The unfamiliar environment, the uncertainty of an illness, and being isolated from family can lead to feelings of fear, anxiety, and deep loneliness.

üìä

of patients experience anxiety symptoms of clinically significant severity

Studies show that higher levels of psychological stress experienced by hospital inpatients are associated with poorer patient outcomes, including increased pain and prolonged recovery time.

This isn't a side effect! It's a critical part of the patient experience that demands our attention and compassion.
